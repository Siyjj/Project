Maps

Maps, as the title suggests, are the explorable places that players are teleported to survive in from the Monsters. All of them each having their own objective/s to do. Some are better with specific killers, while some aren't. A perfect example would be EXE, who does well on maps like Fazbear's Fright but does not do well on Forest.

The Lobby

itself contains multiple interactable content,
having multiple buttons on the bottom, those buttons being the Shop, R$ Shop, Settings, Profile, Ready/AFK,

Oni's Mansion

, also known as simply Mansion, is one of the multiple maps in Pillar Chase 2.
It is inspired by the layout seen in the first Ao Oni game, although taking inspiration of an earlier version of the map,
seen in Version 3.0. It is the signature of Ao Oni. This map was released alongside 4 others when the game released.

The Dungeon

(also referred to as just Dungeon) is one of the multiple maps in-game.
Its appearance is based off of Amnesia: The Dark Decent as well as Terrorift, which fittingly,
is also one of the darkest maps in the game. This map was released alongside 4 others when the game had first released on April 27th, 2024.

The Joy of Creation

(shortened to TJOC) is one of the playable maps in Pillar Chase 2. Based off its name,
it is obviously a reference to the FNAF fan-game of the same title.
This map was released alongside 4 others when the game had first released, on April 27th, 2024.

Baldi's Schoolhouse

(also referred to as just Schoolhouse), is one of the multiple maps in Pillar Chase 2.
It is a smaller version of the schoolhouse in Baldi's Basics in Education and Learning, made by
It is the map made for Baldi, being released alongside him on May 3rd, 2024.

Fazbear's Fright

is one of the many maps of the game, it heavily replicates Fazbear's Fright
from Five Nights At Freddy's 3 and is the signature map for Springtrap.
The map was made by PhoenixFinch, and was added alongside the release of Springtrap on May 31st, 2024.

Hide 'N Seek

(also referred to as Hide and Seek or Hide And Seek 2013) is a map in Pillar Chase 2,
made by Combive. It references the obscure 2013 horror game of the same name.

The Ward

is a dark, decrepit house/hospital with chairs and tables everywhere.
There is also an outside area, which is similar to a hedge maze. The inside of the map is really dirty and dark,
making it good for monsters who are darkly coloured..

Gobble Run

(previously known as Pac-Man) is a map in Pillar Chase 2, made by Combive.
It is an almost 1-1 clone of the map in the original Pac-Man game.

The Forest

(also referred to as just Forest) is a map that is a port from the original
Pillar Chase map of the same name. This map was added to Pillar Chase 2 on May 29th, 2024.

Pillar Drainage System

(or sometimes referred to as Sewers) is one of the multiple maps in-game that has a large,
wide area with multiple pillars, vaults and buttons around it,
containing as well a underneath area that connects to the other 4 sides of the map.

The Pillars

(also referred to as just Pillars), the titular map of Pillar Chase 2,
is one of the multiple maps that has a large wide area, with many pillars, vents and buttons scattered throughout it,
it is one of the darkest maps as there is barely any lights around it.
This map was released alongside 4 others when the game had first released on April 27th, 2024.

My World

(also known as Green Hill Zone) is a map in Pillar Chase 2, made by Combive,
with its prop design being made by ow_dud. It is a deserted version of Green Hill Zone from the Sonic the Hedgehog series,
set in a sunny, red tinted environment. It is the map made for, and most associated with the monster PCX.

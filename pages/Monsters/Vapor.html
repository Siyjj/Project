Monsters

Vapor




Vapor is a muscular, demonic figure that resembles a humanoid being. Vapor lacks their left arm and entire lower body. He is a monster who originates from the first Pillar Chase. He is directly inspired by the main antagonist of the Horror Shooter game, Vapour. He is introduced as the very first monster in Pillar Chase 2 and is completely free from the very beginning along with the other starter characters, Rosemary, and Baldi.

When pressing 1 Vapor will teleport to where his dark sphere is.
If a Spider Skull is following a survivor at the moment, he will teleport to the Spider Skull instead.
He will also receive a small speed boost for a short period of time after teleporting.


When pressing 2 Vapor stays still, and sends out a Spider Skull that is constantly highlighted.
It will chase any nearby player and will deal 9 damage each hit.
To get rid of them, the player must repeatedly press E to kick it, causing it to vanish with a loud crunch.
Vapor can use Demonic Shift to teleport to these Spider Skulls if they're chasing a survivor. Only two Spider Skulls can be present simultaneously. If a player is killed by a Spider Skull, they will immediately drop dead without a death animation.
